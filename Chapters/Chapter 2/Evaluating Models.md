# Evaluating Models

## Mean squared error

$$MSE = Average((y_i - \widehat{f}(x_i))^2)$$

> Calculating MSE on the training data is like *stroking the model's ego*. Don't do that! Calculate MSE on test data to have a better estimate of model performance.
